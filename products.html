OIL CHANGES

It is very beneficial and important for all vehicle owners to check their fluids about every other feul fill-up, it may seem a bit excessive, but it also gives you a chance to do a quick visual inspection and to inspect for any other signs of trouble, such as a worn belt, loose parts, and also to check all the other fluids in your vehicle as well.It is recommended to change your oil about every three months or every 4,800km or to every seven months or to every 12,000km. Your oil is the primary lifeblood of your engine.Without it, the mechanical motion inside of your engine would create excessive friction and heat in mere seconds, resulting in catastrophic damage.Basically, the engine would seize up, or the components would break apart.
